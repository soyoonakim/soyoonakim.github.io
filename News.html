PAST EVENTS​

​ October 15th, 2023   Kensemble participated in A Day of Remembrance: Pregnancy and Infant Loss Awareness event held at the Holy Name Medical Center, Teaneck, NJ Kensemble performed one hour of music during this meaningful event. ​

​ ​ September 28th, 2023   Seo Yoon started an internship at American Composers Orchestra, New York, NY Seo Yoon started her first music administration career at American Composers Orchestra. She is working as a social media & marketing intern, under a guidance of Lyndsay Werking, the Director of Development & Communications of ACO. ​

​ ​ June 21st, 2023   Kensemble held its debut concert at Make Music Englewood, Englewood, NJ Kensemble is a music ensemble based in Bergen County, NJ, dedicated to bringing live music and celebrating Korean culture in Bergen County and beyond. Seo Yoon co-founded the ensemble in April 2023 with five other musicians from Bergen County. Seo Yoon arranges and plays violin in the ensemble, as well as managing the online presence of the ensemble and communicating with collaborators. ​
